Spotify Voice Command Player
  • 1. Click the mic icon
  • 2. Say one of the commands listed below
Available Commands
Play <song>
Search Spotify for the given song and then play it.
Play <song> by <artist>
Search Spotify for the given song by the given artist and then play it.
Play some <genre>
Search Spotify for a song of the given genre and then play it.
Play a song by <artist>
Search Spotify for a song by the given artist and then play it.
Play something like <artist>
Search Spotify for a song of an artist that is similar to the given artist and then play it.
Stop
Pause the current song.
Resume
Resume the current song.
